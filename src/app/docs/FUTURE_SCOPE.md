# Future Scope: Bias Buster Evolution

The future evolution of Bias Buster involves moving beyond static text-based analysis to incorporate multi-modal intelligence, such as real-time audio sentiment detection and video-based non-verbal cue analysis. By leveraging more advanced Genkit flows and multimodal Gemini models, the platform will be able to identify subtle micro-expressions or tone shifts that might indicate candidate stress or interviewer bias as they happen. This progression will transform the tool from a post-interview audit system into a live coaching assistant, providing recruiters with immediate, nudging feedback during the conversation to ensure they remain objective and supportive throughout the interview process.

Beyond technical enhancements, the long-term vision for the platform includes deep integration with global Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and professional networking APIs to provide a holistic, end-to-end fair hiring ecosystem. By correlating interview scores with long-term employee performance and retention data, Bias Buster can begin to offer predictive analytics on which behavioral traits best correlate with success in specific organizational cultures. Ultimately, this creates a data-driven feedback loop that helps companies not just hire fairly, but strategically refine their competency frameworks to build truly equitable and high-performing diverse teams.
