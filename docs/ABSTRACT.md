# Project Abstract: Bias Buster

In modern talent acquisition, unconscious bias remains a significant barrier to equitable hiring, often leading to subjective decisions based on cognitive shortcuts rather than merit. Traditional interview processes frequently lack structured evidence, making it difficult to audit and improve objectivity. The Bias Buster platform addresses this challenge by providing a data-driven framework that transforms raw interview dialogue into structured, actionable intelligence, ensuring that every candidate is evaluated against consistent behavioral standards.

Technically, the platform leverages a sophisticated multi-tier architecture powered by Next.js 15 and React 19 for a high-performance frontend, integrated with Firebase for secure, real-time data persistence. The core intelligence tier utilizes Genkit to orchestrate advanced Large Language Models, specifically Gemini Pro, to perform automated diarization and STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) evidence extraction. By enforcing strict JSON schemas via Zod, the system delivers precise behavioral mapping and flags potential biases such as the framing effect and affinity bias with specific transcript references for total transparency.

The implementation of Bias Buster signifies a shift toward objective, auditable recruitment practices that empower HR professionals to make high-stakes decisions with confidence. By grounding candidate scores in verifiable evidence and providing a critical audit of the interview dynamic, the platform effectively levels the playing field for diverse talent. Looking ahead, the system is architected for continuous evolution, with the potential to integrate real-time sentiment analysis and cross-platform ATS benchmarking to build a more inclusive and high-performing global workforce.
